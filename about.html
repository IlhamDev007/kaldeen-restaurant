About Us

A local favourite in Kalmunai, Sri Lanka — serving fresh, home-style breakfast, lunch, and short eats with love and care every single day.

Who We Are

Kaldeen Restaurant is a trusted local food business in Kalmunai, Sri Lanka, located near Amman Kovil Road. We serve fresh, delicious meals to families, workers, and daily customers in the community — cooked with care every morning.

What We Serve

We offer freshly prepared breakfast, hearty lunch, and crispy short eats to suit our customers’ daily needs.

  • Morning Breakfast — Parotta, Ulundhu Vadai, Dhosai, Idiyappam
  • Lunch — Chicken Biryani, Rice & Curry
  • Short Eats — Rolls, Cutlet, Samosas & Milk Tea

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide every customer with tasty food, good service, fair prices, and a welcoming experience. We are committed to being Kalmunai’s most trusted and loved restaurant — one fresh meal at a time.
